title: "There Is Also Black"
---

![image](https://gyazo.com/e484438fce5ec26a7d0df3cdddb410a4/thumb/1000)

Regarding the same subject:
- Person A says, "It's white."
- Person B says, "There is also black."
- Person A thinks, "Person B is a fool who doesn't even realize it's white."
- Person B thinks, "It's obvious that most of it is white, but what's important is that 'it's not all white; there is also black.' Person A, who thinks everything is white, is a fool."
