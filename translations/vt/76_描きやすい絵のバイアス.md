- [[Two-Dimensional Standard Normal Distribution]] is difficult to draw by hand
    - <img src='https://scrapbox.io/api/pages/nishio/二次元標準正規分布/icon' alt='Two-Dimensional Standard Normal Distribution.icon' height="19.5"/>
    - Often represented as a circle

- When representing a group of people with a circle, it leads to the misconception that there is a "[[Clear Boundary]]"
    - ![image](https://gyazo.com/bbad39d3302e15c6c48683160f8e80f5/thumb/1000)
    - [[Organizational Boundaries]]

- This can also be referred to as a "[[False Dichotomy]]"
    - Believing there is a clear boundary between "inside" and "outside" when there isn't one.

- Instead of just making a page with this single example of "Bias of Easily Drawn Diagrams," I want to think of a few more examples
