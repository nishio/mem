title: "Uncertainty in Expanding Processes"

---

![image](https://gyazo.com/f4f39a38ff820da77246ac47329e25b5/thumb/1000)

In an [[Expanding Process]], a slight lead A at time t1 can become a significant difference B at a later time t2. Predicting whether it will exceed the threshold C at a future time becomes challenging.

What appears to be an [[Exponential]] curve is actually a superposition of probabilistically occurring [[Step Functions]].
