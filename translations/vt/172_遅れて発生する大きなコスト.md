The Large Cost That Occurs Later

![image](https://gyazo.com/c55ccca9cd296e6685c175584802f68d/thumb/1000)

- When faced with the choice between an option that incurs a small cost early and one that incurs a large cost later, B, who sees the whole picture, prefers the option with the early small cost.
- However, A, who has a myopic view, dislikes the early small cost and prefers the option that incurs a large cost later, as it is outside their field of vision.
