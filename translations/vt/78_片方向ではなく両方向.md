title: "Not One-Way, But Two-Way"

![image](https://gyazo.com/108cfb9a159c03a30db7b1ecdad99338/thumb/1000)

- I thought A was providing X to B.
- When I specifically listed what X was, I realized that there are cases where A also receives something.
- This means that when I drew a one-way arrow, I had the assumption that it was one-way.
