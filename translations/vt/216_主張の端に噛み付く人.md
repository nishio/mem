title: "Biting at the Edges of an Argument"
---

![image](https://gyazo.com/57f68098e624357aa1d7db5b86db09eb/thumb/1000)
- Thinking they are refuting what the other person said
- However, it is not the core of the argument, merely [[trivial details]]
    - [[Nitpicking]]
    - [[Scope of Observation Issue]]
    - Biting at the [[edges of the argument]]
- The person being refuted thinks, "It doesn't matter"
    - The person who refuted gets angry, thinking, "I refuted it, but they aren't taking it seriously"
