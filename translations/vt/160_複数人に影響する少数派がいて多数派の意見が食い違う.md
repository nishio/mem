- A Variant of [[Series of Drawings Where Two People Say Different Things]]
    - Could it be [[Series of Drawings Where Groups Say Different Things]]?
    - This phenomenon itself can occur under other conditions
        - When there is a difference in the number of people in groups
            - ![image](https://gyazo.com/b86230c4aeb6476960b6f4abd26a33e2/thumb/1000)
        - When information sharing is active in one group
            - ![image](https://gyazo.com/ed9a7c59e2820940d93753fdf1d4dd54/thumb/1000)
            - For example, this can happen when the victimized side finds it easy to talk to others about being wronged, saying things like "I can't believe they did this, it's so infuriating," while the perpetrating side finds it difficult to talk to others.
