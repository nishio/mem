Variability in Quality Is Not a Bad Thing
- Greater variability results in more ideas scoring above 90 points

![image](https://gyazo.com/25c0c0e4c1238e6a3175ab39013cbee5/thumb/1000)
Variability in Quality Is Not a Good Thing Either
- Greater variability also results in more failing ideas scoring below 60 points

![image](https://gyazo.com/69402d0eda3e902f69aeddcef84b1988/thumb/1000)
The Line to Surpass
- Scoring above 60 is good; scoring above 90 is better
- The value of variability depends on the line you need to surpass.
- Which situation are you in now?

[[Blind Spot Card]]47

"Passing if Above the Line" Is a [[Step Function]]
- When dealing with such [[non-linear]] functions, simply changing the [[variance]] can make things better or worse, even if the average remains unchanged
- Most things in the world are non-linear
