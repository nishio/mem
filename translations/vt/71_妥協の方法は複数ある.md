![image](https://gyazo.com/70ea1590f7221e338b1b6a0981911630/thumb/1000)
When the [[ideal]] state cannot be achieved, it is necessary to pull it down to a [[feasible]] area. However, there is [[room for choice]] in deciding where to pull it towards.

Even if people say "That's great" upon hearing an explanation of an ideal state that has not yet been realized, the attributes they think are "great" are unclear. Therefore, after compromising and realizing it, there is a possibility they might say "This is not good."
