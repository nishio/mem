- [[Series of Drawings Where Two People Say Different Things]]
- "A Can Earn!"
    - In reality, in the sense that it is not zero but positive, "can earn"
- "A Cannot Earn!"
    - Compared to another option B, the return for the same amount of time spent is smaller
    - Considered "negligibly small" and thus ignored

This is a variant of [[Does Adding a Cup of Water to a Lake Raise the Water Level?]]
- [[Identifying Non-Zero as the Same]]
- [[Comparison with Zero]]

In the world, there may be [[scams]] or suspicious business solicitations that deliberately guide you into the "[[narrow-minded]]" thinking like A
- It is difficult to prove intent, and they are not saying anything incorrect

Original Source
- I tried STEPN, and today I was able to earn about 15 GST by walking for 15 minutes before work (fact)
    - Looking only at this, "you can earn by running" is a fact
    - Facts that people who want to give the impression of profitability do not mention
        - 15 GST is about 50 yen in Japanese currency
        - This uses 75% of the energy obtainable in one day
    - Facts that people who want to give the impression of profitability might add
        - This is a story of a user who has just started, less than a day since the game began, and if you level up the in-game equipment, you can earn more efficiently
