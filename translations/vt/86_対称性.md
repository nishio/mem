![image](https://gyazo.com/028921d32b78a30d9526c95b12aa1c44/thumb/1000)  
The hypothesis that if there is something on one side of an axis, there might be a corresponding element on the opposite side of the axis. Refer to [[Place of Nothingness]].  
The right diagram shows enantiomers. Objects in the mirror may appear similar at first glance, but they can become things that cannot be superimposed by rotation.
