> [nishio](https://x.com/nishio/status/1805055782981186012) Some people say, "There will be those left behind with the introduction of digital tools!" However, the fundamental premise is that "the increase in the elderly population and the decrease in the working population is a certain future, and maintaining the current system will lead to a deterioration in service quality due to labor shortages." Therefore, "it is necessary to improve systems through digital tools."
